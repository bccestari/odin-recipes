Hot Dog Recipe

Description

These hot dog sandwiches are baked and kid-approved! Experiment and use whatever combo of ingredients you wish.

Ingredients

  • 8 hot dogs, chopped
  • â…” cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoons pickle relish
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons prepared mustard
  • 3 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 8 hot dog buns

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).

  2. Stir hot dogs, Cheddar cheese, relish, ketchup, mustard, and onion together in a bowl; spoon into hot dog buns. Wrap each sandwich in aluminum foil.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until hot, about 20 minutes. Serve immediately.
